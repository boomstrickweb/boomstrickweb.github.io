Our Framework

Three pillars of sustainable design.

Liora isn't a checklist — it's an integrated design philosophy applied to every product decision we make.

01

Energy-Efficient Code

We profile every feature for computational efficiency. Leaner code means less CPU time, less power, less emissions — and faster software as a side effect.

02

Longevity by Design

Products designed to last reduce the demand for new devices. Liora sets compatibility standards that keep older hardware supported for longer.

03

Transparent Accountability

We publish our full carbon data quarterly — no selective reporting, no marketing spin. Liora partners commit to the same radical transparency.
