About Us

Built for builders,
powered by purpose.

Boomstrick was founded on a simple belief: managing multiple apps shouldn't mean managing multiple headaches. We built one platform to rule them all — and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

Simplify the complexity behind every great product.

The best teams in the world shouldn't spend their energy stitching together disconnected tools. They should spend it building things people love.

Boomstrick started as a side project in 2019, born out of frustration with fragmented app stacks. Today it's the operating system for over 12 million users across 180 countries — and we still believe that unified is always better.

We're a remote-first company, 340 strong, with offices in San Francisco, London, and Singapore. Our north star hasn't changed: one platform, infinite possibility.

Our Story

How we got here

2019

Side project becomes a company

James and Anika launch the first internal prototype of Boomstrick to solve their own multi-app chaos.

2020

Seed round & first 1,000 users

$4M seed from Sequoia. BoomChat launches publicly. The first 1,000 teams sign up in 48 hours.

2021

Series A & BoomSpace launch

$28M Series A. BoomSpace brings collaborative docs and projects into the platform.

2022

BoomShop & BoomGuard ship

Commerce and enterprise security join the platform. 500K users across 80 countries.

2024

Series B — $90M

BoomFlow launches. The automation engine processes 1B runs in its first quarter.

2026

Boomstrick 3.0 & Series C

AI-native platform rebuild. $220M raised. 12M users. 180 countries. The journey continues.
