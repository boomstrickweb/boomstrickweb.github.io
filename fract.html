What Makes FRACT Different?

Built against the grain.

Every design decision we made was a reaction to what the rest of the industry got wrong — deliberately.

✓

No engagement farming

FRACT removes public like counts, repost metrics, and follower numbers from the interface. You post because you mean it — not to chase a number that means nothing.

✓

No addictive design tricks

No infinite scroll traps. No variable-reward notification pulses. No urgency manufactured by an algorithm. FRACT respects your attention instead of exploiting it.

✓

No data exploitation

We don't build ad profiles, sell behavioral data, or share your activity with third parties — now or ever. Your usage is yours.

✓

Automatic AI-content labeling

Know what's generated and what's human. FRACT surfaces AI-generated content clearly — so you always know what's real and what isn't.
